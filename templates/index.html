Welcome, Tournament Admin!

Track and manage Fighters as they battle for Prizes and, of course, GLORY. This tool supports all of your administrative needs for managing a tournament between fictional Fighters.

  • Fighters Allows users to see existing Fighters or create a new Fighter. Fighter profiles for existing Fighters are planned to link to that Fighter's overall win results.
  • Weapons Allows users to look up an existing Weapon or create a new Weapon.
  • Fights Allows lookup and creation/modification of Fights (who is participating, when the Fight is). Allows assignment of Prizes to Fights and assignment of Fight winners.
  • Prizes Allows users to see existing Prizes or create a new Prize.
  • Fighter Wins Contains leaderboard (Fighters with most wins) and allows users to look up a Fighter's wins and Prizes. {% endblock %}
